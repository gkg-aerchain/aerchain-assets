Standard RFP

End-to-end demonstration from event initiation to award. Intake arrives as a system-triggered event, not a manual form. AI drafts the RFP, pulls the supplier shortlist, and pre-loads J&J compliance sections.
Live demo sequence
Intake & AI setup
PR trigger from SAP lands in Aerchain. Aera AI drafts RFP content. Compliance gating pre-loaded: NDA clickwrap, COI upload, quality declarations.
Supplier engagement
Auto-populated shortlist from Supplier Master. sApp supplier portal: bid submission, document exchange, Q&A, real-time activity tracking.
Evaluation & award
Multi-stakeholder scoring with configurable weighting. AI bid analysis. Savings calculation vs incumbent baseline. Award triggers SAP writeback and CLM contract.
Integration touch points
SAP ERP
Requisition/award handoff. PR arrives via IDoc/ALE, award writes back via RFC. Full bidirectional sync.
ServiceNow / IRIS
Dual role: intake source (buyer creates case that triggers sourcing) and downstream exception routing on anomalies.
Contract lifecycle management
Award triggers contract initiation in CLM with terms, pricing, scope, and supplier details via API.
Supplier master
Vendor sync pulling qualified supplier data, segmentation, and historical performance into event setup.

Advanced Sourcing

Beyond basic RFPs. Scenario-based optimization, TCO modeling, constraint solving, and AI-driven decision support for complex, strategic categories.
Scenario modeling & optimization
Multiple award structures side by side. Constraint-based solver optimizing across price, quality, risk, and capacity. Model "what-if" scenarios before committing.
Total cost of ownership
TCO modeling with landed cost variables -- freight, duties, quality risk premiums, lead time costs. Not just unit price comparison.
Reporting & data extraction
Custom analytics dashboards. Power BI / Tableau integration via API. Bid activity reports, supplier coverage, savings attribution.
AI-driven insights
Natural language querying of sourcing data. AI-generated bid summaries. Predictive analytics for savings and supplier risk scoring.
