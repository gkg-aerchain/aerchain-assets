# 💰 Stage 7 — Commercial Proposal + Contract

> **Duration:** 2-6 weeks (depending on legal review cycles) | **Owner:** Sales Lead + Legal
> **Goal:** Proposal accepted, scope locked, contract signed

---

## 📋 Commercial Proposal Structure

| Section | Pages | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Executive Summary | 2 | Value proposition, key outcomes, investment |
| 2. Scope | 5 | Inclusions (P0/P1), Exclusions (P2/P3), Client responsibilities, RACI |
| 3. Pricing Breakdown | 3 | Product (credits + users) + Services (implementation + training + on-site) |
| 4. Services Detail | 3 | Rate card (per role/day), on-site (30 days included, then $X/day), training |
| 5. Implementation Roadmap | 2 | 4 phases, 16 weeks, milestones |
| 6. Governance | 1 | Weekly calls, monthly steering committee, escalation path |
| 7. Success Metrics | 1 | 3 measurable outcomes from Stage 3 + tracking cadence |
| 8. Payment Terms | 1 | Annual / Quarterly / Milestone-based |
| 9. Legal | Attached | T&Cs + MSA + DPA + SLA + SOW |

---

## 💲 Pricing Control Mechanism

> ⚠️ **CRITICAL PHILOSOPHY:** *"Put a price to everything. 80% of asks disappear."*

| Customer Ask | Response |
|---|---|
| Custom field | "$5-10K per field, +2 weeks timeline" |
| Extended on-site | "$200-400/day after month 1" |
| Additional integration | "$20-50K, +4 weeks" |
| Extra training | Rate card pricing |

**Mechanism:** Change Request Order Form → Instant pricing + timeline impact → Customer approves/declines

---

## 🤝 Negotiation Levers (If Pricing Pushback)

| Lever | Details |
|---|---|
| ✅ Payment terms flexibility | Annual → Quarterly → Milestone |
| ✅ Scope reduction | Fewer categories, fewer users, fewer channels |
| ✅ Phased pricing | Year 1 lighter, Year 2 full |
| ✅ Pilot pricing | 3-month pilot at reduced rate, then full license |
| ❌ **NEVER** | Discount without scope reduction. *"Every dollar removed = feature/service removed."* |

## 🔷 Exit Gate

> **"Contract signed?"**
> ✅ **YES** → Stage 8 (Implementation Handover)
> ⏳ **Legal delays** → Weekly check-ins, escalate CEO-to-CEO if needed
> ❌ **Budget rejected** → Rework pricing, reduce scope, re-present within 2 weeks
> ❌ **Competitor won** → Capture loss reasons, park 12 months, re-engage at renewal

---

## 🔄 Nurture Track 7

| Activity | Details |
|---|---|
| Executive negotiation | CEO-to-CEO for deals >$500K |
| Payment terms flexibility | Restructure payment schedule |
| Scope reduction + re-pricing | Remove non-essentials |
| Pilot proposal | 3-month proof at reduced investment |

**Timeline:** 4-6 weeks active negotiation → hard decision

**Disqualify:** Budget permanently rejected → park for next fiscal year
