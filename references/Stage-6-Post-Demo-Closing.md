# 🏁 Stage 6 — Post-Demo Closing Motion — Multi-Threaded Sales Execution

> **Duration:** 4-8 weeks | **Owner:** Sales Lead + Full Team
> **Goal:** Secure internal approval across all stakeholders through 6 parallel workstreams

> ⚠️ **THIS IS THE MOST CRITICAL STAGE.** The period between "they want to buy" and "they've committed to buy." Enterprise deals are won or lost here.

---

## 🧵 6 Parallel Threads Overview

> 💡 These threads run **IN PARALLEL**, not sequentially. The sales team must manage all threads simultaneously.

| Thread | Name | Duration | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| **6A** | Champion Enablement | Week 1-2 | Sales Lead |
| **6B** | Multi-Stakeholder Threading | Week 1-6 | Sales + Solutions + Delivery |
| **6C** | IT/Security Evaluation | Week 1-8 | Solutions Architect |
| **6D** | Reference Calls | Week 2-4 | Sales + Customer Success |
| **6E** | Executive Alignment | Week 3-6 | CEO (Harsha/Gaurav) |
| **6F** | POC/Pilot Decision | Week 4-8 | Sales Lead (conditional) |

---

## 6A: Champion Enablement (Week 1-2)

> 💡 *"Your champion is your internal sales team. If they can't sell AerChain when you're NOT in the room, the deal dies."*

### Champion Enablement Checklist

- [ ] Internal business case deck — **NOT your sales deck.** THEIR deck, in THEIR language, with THEIR numbers from L2/L3
- [ ] ROI summary one-pager for CFO — *"Here's the financial case: $28M Year 1 savings, 8-month payback, 12.5x 3-year ROI"*
- [ ] Technical architecture summary for CIO
- [ ] "Why Now" arguments — Cost of delay per month, competitive risk, fiscal year timing
- [ ] Top 10 internal objection answers — Pre-scripted responses
- [ ] Champion enablement kit — Complete toolkit: talking points, data sheets, competitor comparison, case studies

---

## 6B: Multi-Stakeholder Threading (Week 1-6)

> 💡 *"You cannot close an enterprise deal selling to one person."*

| Stakeholder | Their Concern | AerChain Lead | Key Collateral | Key Message |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **CPO / VP Sourcing** | Adoption, team impact, process change | Sales Lead | Platform demo, case studies | "Your team does 4-5x more strategic work" |
| **CFO / Finance** | ROI, TCO, payback, budget classification | Sales + BA | L3 ROI model, financial case | "8-month payback, 12.5x 3-year ROI" |
| **CIO / IT** | Security, architecture, integration | Solutions Architect | Security brief, architecture diagram | "SOC2 Type II, NSE-cleared, 16-week integration" |
| **CEO / MD** | Strategic vision, competitive advantage | CEO (Harsha/Gaurav) | Executive brief, transformation narrative | "Procurement as competitive advantage" |
| **Procurement Leads** | Day-to-day usage, learning curve | Presales Engineer | User testimonials, training roadmap | "Cars24: 99% adoption. Makes your job easier." |
| **Legal** | MSA, DPA, liability, IP, SLAs | Sales + Legal | Contract templates, compliance docs | "Standard MSA, GDPR-compliant DPA" |

## 6C: IT / Security Evaluation (Week 1-8)

> ⚠️ **#1 deal blocker in enterprise sales.** Start as EARLY as possible — ideally during Stage 2/3.

### IT/Security Checklist

- [ ] Security questionnaire responses (200-500 questions, pre-filled templates, 1-2 week turnaround)
- [ ] Architecture review session with IT team
- [ ] SOC2 Type II report sharing
- [ ] ISO27001 certification documentation
- [ ] GDPR compliance documentation + DPA template
- [ ] Penetration testing results (if required)
- [ ] Data residency and encryption discussion
- [ ] Vendor risk assessment

> 💬 Reference: *"NSE cleared us after 6-7 months of the most rigorous security evaluation in Indian financial services — only cloud provider approved."*

---

## 6D: Reference Calls (Week 2-4)

### Reference Call Types

| Type | Format | When to Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Peer-to-Peer** (CPO to CPO) | Cars24/Anupam: "99% adoption in 3 months" | Champion needs validation |
| **Practitioner-to-Practitioner** | Day-to-day experience, UI, adoption | Procurement team skeptical |
| **IT-to-IT** | Integration, security eval, performance | CIO blocking |
| **CFO-to-CFO** | Manjushree/Ayanish: "4.5% savings" | Budget is the barrier |

### Reference Call Protocol
1. **Pre-brief** the reference: Share context, what prospect cares about
2. **Prepare** the prospect: Set expectations on who they'll speak with
3. **Facilitate** the call: AerChain in listen mode — let reference do the talking
4. **Follow up** within 24 hours: Capture feedback, address concerns

---

## 6E: Executive Alignment (Week 3-6)

**When to deploy:** Deal size $500K+, or when CPO alone cannot approve

- CEO-to-CEO conversation (Harsha/Gaurav → Client CEO/MD)
- Board-ready narrative (1-page strategic vision)
- Industry transformation context ("What leading companies are doing")
- Competitive urgency ("Cost of inaction")

---

## 6F: POC/Pilot Decision (Week 4-8, Conditional)

**When to offer:** Client cannot commit to full deal without proof

| POC Type | Duration | Scope | Investment |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Technical POC** | 2-4 weeks | 1 category, limited users | Reduced fee |
| **Pilot** | 3 months | Wave 1 (tail spend), full workflow | Discounted Year 1 |

> ⚠️ POC scope must be clearly defined: success criteria, timeline, what happens after.
