# ⭐ The 10 Commandments of AerChain Sales

> These are non-negotiable. Every team member, every deal, every stage.

| # | Commandment |
|---|---|
| **1** | *"Follow the Bible. We can make the Bible better, but don't do anything outside the Bible."* |
| **2** | *"What we do, what we don't do — clearly defined at every stage."* |
| **3** | *"Sales discusses one-tenth. Scope for going wrong is one-tenth. Implementation discusses everything. Control BOTH."* |
| **4** | *"Services must add up to the value story."* |
| **5** | *"Include implementation in the workshop. Shows we care about delivery, not just signing."* |
| **6** | *"Put a price to everything. 80% of asks disappear."* |
| **7** | *"We are a 5-star company. Give the 5-star experience."* |
| **8** | *"Standard pricing across all deals. No one uses their brain."* |
| **9** | *"Structured discovery — 50 points, 25 pre-filled. We come prepared."* |
| **10** | *"Handover is on the giving person. Sales owns the handover quality."* |
