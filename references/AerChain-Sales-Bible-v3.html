STAGE 0
🎯
Lead Generation & Market Positioning
Ongoing  ·  Owner: Marketing + SDR + Partnerships
6 Collaterals
5 Input Channels
  • 0A Cold Outbound — CPOs, VP Sourcing. Research → L1 biz case → email snapshot. 5–8% conversion
  • 0B Inbound — Website, content, whitepaper. 15–20% MQL→SQL
  • 0C Events + CEO Dinners — Custom L1 for ALL attendees. Print as brochures. 10–15% → call
  • 0D Partner Referrals — Deloitte, Infosys, Accenture, EY. 25–30% warm conversion
  • 0E Customer Referrals — Existing champion network. 30–40% (highest quality)
Pre-Work Checklist
  • Research: annual report, SEC filings, procurement maturity
  • Build L1 business case (60% accurate) — industry benchmarks + estimated spend
  • Identify likely pain points based on vertical + company size
  • Stakeholder map: CPO, CFO, CIO, procurement leads
  • Competitive intel: what systems they currently use (Ariba? Coupa? In-house?)
Stage 0 Collaterals (9 Items)
  • L1 Business Case (industry-specific)
  • Printed brochure (1-pager for events)
  • Email snapshot (teaser)
  • Industry benchmarking report
  • "Sentient Procurement" whitepaper
  • Competitive positioning 1-pager
  • Customer case studies (Cars24, WeWork, ABInBev, Danone)
  • Security brief (SOC2 Type II, ISO27001, GDPR)
  • Co-branded partner solution briefs
Key Insight
Custom L1 for each company at the event is the differentiator. Not generic brochures. Procurement is a close-knit industry. One success story unlocks 10 deals.
🔷
Exit Gate: Prospect agrees to discovery call? ✅ YES → Stage 1  |  ❌ NO → Nurture Track 0 (thought leadership + L1 refresh + events, 6–12 months)
STAGE 1
📞
Discovery Call + L1 Business Case
45–60 min  ·  Owner: Sales Lead (Harsha/Gaurav)
9 Collaterals
Opening — Choose Register
  • CPO / VP Sourcing: "We were the end user. We managed procurement for 50+ enterprises. Handled 5 crores/month, 150–200 orders/day. We saw every tool fail. We built AerChain to be what those tools should have been."
  • MD / CFO / CIO: "Procurement is a business support function. Today it operates like a ticketing system — high volume, low value. What if we moved your team from 'processor' to 'policy owner and optimizer'?"
Problem Diagnosis Questions
  • "How many sourcing events per year/week?"
  • "What's your average sourcing cycle time, PR to PO?"
  • "What systems today? Ariba? Coupa? In-house?"
  • "Biggest pain — cycle time? Vendor adoption? Compliance? Cost?"
  • "Who initiates spend? How do they do it today?"
  • "How do you handle strategic vs. tactical vs. routine sourcing?"
4 Value Pillars
  • 🏋️ Productivity — 80% grunt work eliminated. 4–5x more strategic work. Reliance: 300+ events/week
  • ⏱️ Cycle Time — Sourcing 60→25 days. PR-to-PO 45→15 days. 67% reduction
  • 💰 Financial Impact — 1%+ savings from market intel, consolidation, efficiency
  • 🤖 Future-Proofing — AI-native. Every transaction learns. NSE cleared after 6–7 months security eval
Stage 1 Collaterals
  • Discovery call agenda (1-pager)
  • L1 business case presentation (25–30 slides)
  • Origin story script (3-min, two registers)
  • Platform overview deck (5 pillars + 5 agents + 9 pathways)
  • Competitive positioning 1-pager
  • Security/compliance fact sheet
  • Pricing overview 1-pager (high-level)
  • Next-step proposal (workshop date template)
  • Recorded demo walkthrough (async option)
Top Objection Responses
  • "Different from Ariba/Coupa?" — "Not here to replace Ariba. Tell me an area that feels difficult. Try AerChain for that. We integrate."
  • "Can your AI really do this?" — "Human-in-the-loop always. AI assists, you decide. Gets better with every transaction."
  • "What does this cost?" — "Consumption-based, starts at $50K+. For every $1 invested, $8–10 savings Year 1. 8-month payback."
  • "Small company, can you deliver?" — "Reliance trusts us. NSE cleared us after 6–7 months security eval. 80+ team, $20B+ spend managed."
🔷
Exit Gate: Client agrees to deep-dive demo? ✅ YES → Stage 2 (propose date within 2 weeks)  |  ❌ NO → Nurture Track 1 (recorded demo + biz case updates + reference calls, 3–6 months)

STAGE 3
🏢
Workshop + L2 Business Case — Value Engineering & Scope Lock
3 days on-site (preferred)  ·  Owner: Full AerChain Team (5 people)
13 Collaterals
Day 1 — Discovery
  • 50-point structured discovery questionnaire — 25 PRE-FILLED from Stage 0/1 research. "We come prepared."
  • Current process walk: PR→sourcing→evaluation→negotiation→award→PO→payment. Count steps, hours, people.
  • Stakeholder interviews (30 min each): Procurement, Finance, IT, Business Users
  • Pain point quantification in $: cycle time, maverick spend, vendor adoption, audit rework
Day 2 — Value Stream
  • Value stream map: Current 50+ steps, 60+ days → AerChain 15–20 steps, 20–30 days
  • Custom demo with THEIR thresholds, THEIR T&Cs, THEIR categories, THEIR vendor types
  • Integration architecture: AerChain ↔ ERP (SAP/Oracle) ↔ CLM ↔ Vendor master
Day 3 — Business Case
  • L2 Business Case (80% accurate): 3-year financial model, ROI, IRR, payback
  • 3 Measurable Outcomes LOCKED ⭐ — Both sides sign off. Not aspirational. Achievable. Trackable.
  • 9 Savings Levers: Demand Aggregation 4–7%, Contract Compliance 3–6%, Category Consolidation 3–7%, PPV 3–6%, Supplier Rationalization 3–5%, Channel Optimization 1–3%. Net: 2–3% baseline → 6–8% with AerChain
  • Wave-based rollout: Wave 1 (tail spend), Wave 2 (indirect), Wave 3 (direct/strategic)
🔷
Exit Gate: L2 accepted + scope agreed? ✅ YES + large deal ($500K+) → Stage 4 (Spend Analysis)  |  ✅ YES + standard deal → Stage 5 or Stage 6  |  ❌ NO → Nurture Track 3 (exec escalation, 1–2 months)
STAGE 4
📊
Spend Analysis + L3 Business Case
2–4 weeks  ·  Conditional: deals $500K+ or CFO requires deeper validation
10 Collaterals
AI Spend Analysis
  • Client uploads 1–2 years of spend data
  • Data quality assessment: completeness, missing fields, duplicates
  • Category breakdown: top 10–20 categories
  • Vendor concentration & dependency analysis
  • Geographic distribution: multi-region spend patterns
  • Maverick spend identification: off-contract, non-compliant
  • Market benchmarking vs. industry rates
Savings Waterfall & L3 Output
  • Market intelligence: 0.3%
  • Vendor consolidation: 0.5%
  • Operational efficiency: 0.2%
  • Total: 1.0%+ conservative
  • Credit consumption model: transactions × events/month × credits/workflow
  • L3 Business Case (90–95% accurate) — 60–100 pages: category strategy, 3–5 year financial model with sensitivities, phased implementation roadmap
🔷
Exit Gate: L3 accepted by all stakeholders? ✅ YES → Stage 5 (Customized Demo) or Stage 6  |  ❌ Numbers questioned → Refine + re-present within 2 weeks

2–6 weeks  ·  Owner: Sales Lead + Legal

  • 2. Scope (5pp) — inclusions P0/P1, exclusions P2/P3, client responsibilities, RACI
  • 3. Pricing Breakdown (3pp) — credits + users + services
  • 4. Services Detail (3pp) — rate card, on-site (30 days included), training
  • 5. Implementation Roadmap (2pp) — 4 phases, 16 weeks, milestones
  • 6. Governance (1p) — weekly calls, monthly steering, escalation
  • 7. Success Metrics (1p) — 3 outcomes from Stage 3
  • 8. Payment Terms, Legal: MSA + DPA + SLA + SOW
Pricing Control & Negotiation Levers
  • ⚠️ CRITICAL: "Put a price to everything. 80% of asks disappear."
  • Custom field → $5–10K per field + 2 weeks timeline
  • Extended on-site → $200–400/day after Month 1
  • Additional integration → $20–50K + 4 weeks
  • ✅ Levers: payment terms flexibility, scope reduction, phased pricing, pilot pricing
  • ❌ NEVER discount without scope reduction. "Every dollar removed = feature/service removed."

Exit Gate: Contract signed? ✅ YES → Stage 8  |  ⏳ Legal delays → Weekly check-ins, escalate CEO-to-CEO  |  ❌ Budget rejected → Rework pricing, reduce scope, re-present in 2 weeks  |  ❌ Competitor won → Park 12 months, re-engage at renewal

V5 Principles

The V5 Transformation

V1–V4 was configurable platform thinking. V5 is prescriptive product thinking. This changes everything.

🔄
Language shift rule: Never say "platform" (say "product"). Never say "configurable" or "customizable" (say "predefined" or "prescriptive"). Never say "16-week implementation" as a negative (say "day-one value"). Every piece of old content must be updated to V5 language.
— V5 Content Drift Rule · March 2026
01
Product, Not Platform
AerChain is a product with predefined workflows, not a blank canvas. Stop selling configurability — sell day-one value. 5 configurable templates cover all scenarios.
02
Prescriptive, Not Custom
We define the right way to procure. We don't bend to broken processes. "We sell a product, not custom software development." This protects delivery quality.
03
Day-One Value
16 weeks is not a long implementation — it's a structured path to immediate value. Wave 1 delivers ROI in Month 1. Frame this as speed, not duration.
04
AI is the Core, Not a Feature
5 AI agents, Aiera co-pilot, 140+ AI use cases. Not "AI-powered" — AI-native. Every transaction learns. System gets smarter with use. Compliance by design.
05
Orchestration Over Automation
Not just automating tasks — orchestrating spend across 9 intelligent channels. Policy-based routing that adapts to spend complexity, geography, and risk. This is the Spend Operating System.
06
Sell Outcomes, Not Modules
Lead with the business outcome: cycle time, savings %, adoption rate. Modules are how we get there — not the pitch. "65% of tail spend automated in Month 6" beats "here are our sourcing modules."
07
Multi-Threaded by Design
V5 sales process is built for enterprise complexity — 6 parallel closing threads, multi-stakeholder by default. Never single-threaded. Champion + CFO + CIO + CEO must all be active.
08
Price Everything, Always
Every custom ask gets a price and a timeline impact. Communicated instantly. 80% of asks disappear when you do. The rest fund the roadmap. This is V5 commercial discipline.
