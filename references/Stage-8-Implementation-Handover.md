# 🚀 Stage 8 — Implementation Handover

> **Duration:** 1-2 weeks (handover process) | **Owner:** Sales Lead → Delivery Lead
> **Goal:** Seamless transition from Sales to Delivery. Zero information loss.

> ⚠️ **"Handover is on the GIVING person, not the receiving person."** Sales OWNS ensuring Delivery understands scope perfectly.

---

## 📋 Handover Checklist

- [ ] Signed scope document reviewed together (Sales + Delivery)
- [ ] 3 measurable outcomes confirmed by Delivery lead
- [ ] Client stakeholder map handed over (who to call, who decides, who blocks, who champions)
- [ ] All workshop materials shared (discovery responses, process maps, L2/L3 business case)
- [ ] L3 business case with savings levers and wave-based rollout plan
- [ ] Known risks flagged (data quality, change resistance, IT concerns, vendor adoption risks)
- [ ] Client expectations set: *"This is your delivery team. They own your success now."*
- [ ] **JOINT KICKOFF:** Sales + Delivery + Client together for first meeting
- [ ] Sales stays involved for first 4 weeks (weekly check-ins)
- [ ] Sales transitions out after Month 1 (quarterly business reviews only)
- [ ] Client communication plan agreed
- [ ] Success metrics tracking mechanism confirmed

## 📅 16-Week Implementation (Reference)

> Full details in separate **Implementation Bible**

| Phase | Weeks | Activities | Gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Discovery & Design** | 1-4 | Spend analysis validation, channel design, integration blueprint, user role mapping, governance | Design approved |
| **2. Build & Configure** | 5-10 | Platform setup, workflow config, ERP integration, data migration, training content | System ready |
| **3. Pilot & Test** | 11-13 | Wave 1 pilot, UAT, vendor portal pilot, performance tuning | UAT passed |
| **4. Rollout & Hypercare** | 14-16 | Go-live Wave 1, on-site support, daily standups, issue resolution <4hrs | 70%+ adoption |

## 🔄 What Happens Next

After go-live, the **Customer Success** cycle begins:
- Weekly check-ins (first 4 weeks post go-live)
- Monthly business reviews (outcomes, adoption, issues)
- Quarterly executive reviews (expansion, strategic alignment)

### Expansion Playbook

| Timeline | Action | Upsell |
|---|---|---|
| **Month 3** | "You're live with 2 categories. Here are 3 more." | Additional categories |
| **Month 6** | "You've saved $X. Negotiation agent can add 0.5%." | AI agent upsell |
| **Month 9** | "200 vendors on platform. Autonomous sourcing for 60%." | Autonomous tier |
| **Year 1** | "You achieved [outcomes]. Year 2: all categories, all channels, full AI." | Renewal + growth |
