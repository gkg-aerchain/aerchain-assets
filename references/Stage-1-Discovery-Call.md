# 📞 Stage 1 — Discovery Call + L1 Business Case

> **Duration:** 45-60 minutes | **Owner:** Sales Lead (Harsha/Gaurav)
> **Goal:** Establish credibility, diagnose pain, show high-level capability, lock next step

---

## 🎙️ Call Structure (Scripted Flow)

### [0-3 min] Opening — Choose Language Register

| If Audience Is... | Use This Register |
|---|---|
| **CPO / VP Sourcing / Procurement** | *"We were the end user. We managed procurement for 50+ enterprises. Handled 5 crores/month, 150-200 orders/day. We saw every tool fail. We built AerChain to be what those tools should have been."* |
| **MD / CFO / CIO** | *"Procurement is a business support function. Today it operates like a ticketing system — high volume, low value. What if we could move your team from 'processor' to 'policy owner and optimizer'?"* |
| **Mixed (CPO + CFO)** | Start executive, shift to procurement when CPO engages with questions |

---

### [3-5 min] Credentials (Keep Tight)

- Founded by practitioners (managed procurement for 50+ enterprises)
- Gaurav brought Reliance (India's largest conglomerate)
- **Live with:** Reliance (300+ events/week), Unilever (negotiation agent live), Danone (9.5/10 language), AB InBev (10 countries), NSE (regulated, SOC2-cleared), Meta
- 80+ team, $20B+ spend managed
- 140+ AI agents under 5 themes

---

### [5-15 min] Problem Diagnosis (Interactive, Not Monologue)

Present L1 business case findings:

> 💬 *"We did some research on [company]. Here's what we found: [estimated spend], [estimated events/year], [likely pain points]. Is this directionally accurate?"*

**Client validates or corrects** → Every correction = deeper engagement = better L2 later

**Key questions to ask:**
- "How many sourcing events do you run per year? Per week?"
- "What's your average sourcing cycle time, PR to PO?"
- "What systems are you using today? Ariba? Coupa? In-house?"
- "What's your biggest pain? Cycle time? Vendor adoption? Compliance? Cost?"
- "Who initiates spend in your organization? How do they do it today?"
- "How do you handle strategic vs. tactical vs. routine sourcing today?"

---

### [15-30 min] High-Level Demo (10,000 ft — NOT Deep Dive)

**Show 4 things ONLY:**

| # | What to Show | Key Message |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Aiera Copilot** | Business user types request → AI routes it → right process triggered |
| 2 | **Orchestration Engine** | Based on policies, spend gets routed to the right channel automatically |
| 3 | **RFI → RFP → RFQ flow** | One system, no context loss, journey from discovery to quote |
| 4 | **AI Highlights** | Vendor recommendations, commercial normalization, negotiation strategy |

> ⚠️ **DO NOT show:** Detailed configurations, sealed bids, evaluation workflows, award scenarios (save for Stage 2)

---

### [30-40 min] Value Discussion — 4 Pillars

| Pillar | Pitch | Proof |
|---|---|---|
| **🏋️ Productivity** | "80% of procurement work is grunt. We eliminate it. Your team does 4-5x more strategic work." | Reliance — 300+ events/week at scale |
| **⏱️ Cycle Time** | "Sourcing from 60 days to 25 days. PR-to-PO from 45 to 15-20 days." | Reliance — 67% reduction in PR-to-PO |
| **💰 Financial Impact** | "1%+ savings from market intelligence, consolidation, and operational efficiency." | Industry benchmarks + credit model |
| **🤖 Future-Proofing** | "AI-native. Every transaction learns. System gets smarter. Compliance by design." | NSE — 6-7 month security eval cleared |

---

### [40-45 min] Close — Concrete Next Step (Mandatory)

> ⚠️ **NEVER end with** "Let's see how we proceed"
> ✅ **ALWAYS** propose specific next step with DATE

> 💬 *"Based on what we've discussed, I'd recommend a deep-dive demo with your sourcing team — [propose specific date within 2 weeks]. We'll show the full RFI-to-Award journey customized to your scenarios. Should we lock [date]?"*

**If they resist date:**
> 💬 *"No pressure on timing. Can I send you a recorded demo walkthrough this week? You review async, and we reconnect when you're ready."*

## 🛡️ Objection Handling (Top 5)

### "How is this different from Ariba/Coupa?"

> 💬 *"Not here to replace Ariba. Tell me an area of spend that feels difficult to manage. Try AerChain for that. We integrate."*
> **Close:** "You're buying time-to-value. And compliance by design."

### "Can your AI really do this?"

> 💬 *"Human-in-the-loop always. AI assists, you decide. It gets better with every transaction."*
> **Show:** Audit trail. Unilever's negotiation agent — AI generated strategy, human approved.

### "What does this cost?"

> 💬 *"Consumption-based, starts at $50K+. We'll calculate exactly based on your spend data in the workshop."*
> **Value Context:** "For every $1 you invest, we deliver $8-10 in savings Year 1. 8-month payback."

### "You're a small company, can you deliver?"

> 💬 *"Reliance — India's largest conglomerate — trusts us. NSE cleared us after 6-7 months of security evaluation. 80+ team. $20B+ spend managed."*

### "How fast can you go live?"

> 💬 *"16-week standard implementation in 4 phases. First value in Wave 1 (tail spend) within Q1. ROI visible by Month 6."*
