# 🎯 Stage 0 — Lead Generation & Market Positioning

> **Duration:** Ongoing | **Owner:** Marketing + SDR + Partnerships
> **Goal:** Generate qualified leads and book discovery calls

---

## 📥 5 Input Channels

### 0A: Cold Outbound

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Target** | CPOs, VP Sourcing, Heads of Procurement |
| **Activity** | Research company → build L1 business case → send email snapshot |
| **Collateral** | Email snapshot (1-page value summary + QR to full L1) |
| **Conversion** | 5-8% response rate → discovery call booked |
| **Owner** | SDR team |

### 0B: Inbound (Website / Content)

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Procurement leaders searching for solutions |
| **Activity** | Website inquiry → qualify → route to sales |
| **Collateral** | Website content, "Sentient Procurement" whitepaper, blog posts |
| **Conversion** | 15-20% MQL to SQL |
| **Owner** | Marketing |

### 0C: Events (Conferences / CEO Dinners / Webinars)

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Target** | 100+ companies at industry events (Dubai model) |
| **Activity** | Pre-build custom L1 reports for ALL attendees → print as brochures → hand out at booth |
| **Collateral** | Printed L1 brochure (1-pager per company), event booth materials |
| **Conversion** | 10-15% of attendees → discovery call |
| **Owner** | Marketing + SDR |

> 💡 **KEY INSIGHT:** *"Custom L1 for each company at the event is the differentiator. Not generic brochures."*

### 0D: Partner Referrals (Deloitte, Infosys, Accenture, EY)

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Partner's existing procurement clients |
| **Activity** | Partner identifies client need → introduces Aerchain → co-sell |
| **Collateral** | Co-branded solution brief, partner-specific positioning deck |
| **Conversion** | 25-30% referral to discovery (warm intro) |
| **Owner** | Partnerships team + Partner sales |

### 0E: Customer Referrals / Peer Network

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Prospects within existing customer's industry network |
| **Activity** | Existing champion introduces Aerchain to peer → reference call |
| **Collateral** | Customer success story, reference call script, champion kit |
| **Conversion** | 30-40% (highest quality leads) |
| **Owner** | Customer Success + Sales |

> 💡 **KEY INSIGHT:** *"Procurement is a close-knit industry. One success story unlocks 10 deals."*

---

## ✅ Pre-Work Checklist (Before First Meeting)

- [ ] Research company: Annual report, SEC filings, industry position, procurement maturity
- [ ] Build L1 business case (60% accurate): Industry benchmarks, estimated spend, estimated events/year
- [ ] Identify likely pain points: Based on industry vertical + company size + known systems
- [ ] Stakeholder mapping: Who is CPO, CFO, CIO, procurement team leads
- [ ] Competitive intel: What systems do they currently use (Ariba? Coupa? In-house?)

## 🔷 Exit Gate

> **"Prospect agrees to discovery call?"**
> ✅ **YES** → Move to Stage 1
> ❌ **NO** → Enter Nurture Track 0

---

## 🔄 Nurture Track 0

| Activity | Cadence |
|---|---|
| Monthly thought leadership emails (industry-specific) | Monthly |
| L1 business case refresh (new data, new metrics) | Quarterly |
| Event invitations (CEO dinners, webinars, analyst briefings) | Quarterly |
| Partner-maintained relationship (keep warm through partner) | Ongoing |

**Re-engage Triggers:** New funding round, leadership change, fiscal year start, competitor failure

**Timeline:** 6-12 months before re-engage or disqualify

**Disqualify:** No response after 3 touchpoints over 12 months → archive
