# 🏢 Stage 3 — Workshop + L2 Business Case — Value Engineering & Scope Lock

> **Duration:** 3 days (on-site preferred) | **Owner:** Full AerChain team (5 people)
> **Goal:** Deep discovery, value stream mapping, L2 business case, 3 measurable outcomes locked

---

## 📅 Day 1: Discovery & Current State

### Activity 1: Structured Discovery Questionnaire
- 50 points total, **25 pre-filled** from Stage 0/1 research
- Position: *"We come prepared. Please confirm these. The remaining 25, we discuss."*

### Activity 2: Current Process Walk
- Map every step: PR intake → sourcing → evaluation → negotiation → award → PO → payment
- Count: steps, hours, people, systems involved
- Identify every handoff and bottleneck

### Activity 3: Stakeholder Interviews (30 min each)
| Stakeholder | Key Question |
|---|---|
| Procurement team | "What's your biggest daily pain?" |
| Finance | "Where do you lose money in procurement?" |
| IT | "What integrations are non-negotiable?" |
| Business users | "How do you request spend today?" |

### Activity 4: Pain Point Quantification
| Pain | Quantification |
|---|---|
| Cycle time | X days costs $Y in expedited shipments |
| Maverick spend | X% = $Y in missed savings |
| Vendor adoption | X% = $Y in manual follow-up |
| Audit rework | X hours = $Y per quarter |

## 📅 Day 3: Business Case & Implementation Planning

### Activity 1: L2 Business Case Development (80% Accurate)
- Current state pain (quantified from Day 1)
- AerChain impact per use case
- 3-year financial model with sensitivities
- ROI calculation: Year 1, Year 2, Year 3, IRR, Payback

### Activity 2: 3 Measurable Outcomes (LOCKED ⭐)
> Both sides sign off. Not aspirational. Achievable. Trackable.

**Template:** *"By [specific date], we achieve: (1) ___, (2) ___, (3) ___"*

**Examples:**
- "By Month 6, 65% of tail spend (<$50K) routed through Orchestrator with 4-day cycle time"
- "By Month 9, 50% automation of indirect sourcing, cycle time from 45 → 20 days"
- "By Month 12, 8% procurement efficiency (vs 2% baseline), delivering $12M net savings"

### Activity 3: 9 Savings Levers Preview

| Lever | Typical Upside |
|---|---|
| Demand Aggregation | 4-7% |
| Contract Compliance | 3-6% |
| Category Consolidation | 3-7% |
| Payment Term Compliance | 0.2-0.5% |
| Purchase Price Variance | 3-6% |
| Supplier Rationalization | 3-5% |
| Supplier Payment Term Optimization | 0.5-1.0% |
| Payment Terms Rationalization | 0.3-0.8% |
| Channel Optimization | 1-3% |

**Net Effect:** 2-3% baseline today → **6-8% with AerChain orchestration**

### Activity 4: Wave-Based Category Rollout

| Wave | Timeline | Categories | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Wave 1** | Q1 | Tail Spend (<$50K): office, subscriptions, misc | Prove value, build confidence |
| **Wave 2** | Q2 | Indirect: IT services, marketing, professional services | Scale automation, cycle time wins |
| **Wave 3** | Q3-Q4 | Direct & Strategic: raw materials, equipment | Maximum financial impact |

### Activity 5: Implementation Overview (15-30 min)

| Phase | Weeks | Activities |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Discovery & Design | 1-4 | Technical discovery, process design, user stories |
| 2. Build & Configure | 5-10 | System config, integration, data migration, training content |
| 3. Pilot & Test | 11-13 | Wave 1 pilot, UAT, vendor portal pilot |
| 4. Rollout & Hypercare | 14-16 | Go-live, on-site support, daily standups |

### Activity 6: Scope Document (Draft)
- **Included (P0/P1):** Orchestrator, Aiera, 5 AI agents, RFx workflow, sealed bids, negotiation agent, ERP integration, vendor portal, 16-week implementation
- **Excluded (P2/P3):** CLM, P2P full suite, supply chain visibility, SRM, advanced BI, mobile vendor app
