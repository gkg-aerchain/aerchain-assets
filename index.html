🎬 The 16-Step Demo Flow

Step 1: Aiera Copilot Front Door

"Business user types or speaks: 'I need 100 units of X for Project Y'" → Aiera understands, guides, recommends channel

⭐ WOW: Natural language → structured procurement request. No forms. No menus.

Step 2: Orchestration Engine

"Based on YOUR policies — spend, category, complexity, geography — system routes automatically" → Show 9 channels

⭐ WOW: Automatic channel routing. Policy enforcement invisible.

Step 3: RFI Creation

Template-based, AI-assisted content. Vendor recommendations from historical spend (1-3 years)

⭐ WOW: Click "AI Assist" → Aiera generates entire questionnaire section. 15 min instead of 2 days.

Step 4: Vendor Experience

Simple guided interface. Download to Excel → work offline → upload back. Chat for clarifications.

⭐ WOW: Vendor does everything in 15 minutes, not 2 days.

Step 5: AI Technical Analysis

AI reads EVERYTHING vendors submitted → Company profile, strengths, weaknesses, red flags, document summaries

⭐ WOW: "AI did in 2 minutes what takes your team 2 days"

Step 6: Email Deliverability Tracking

Sent → Delivered → Opened → Clicked (full tracking chain per vendor)

⭐ WOW: "NSE complained vendors don't receive emails. This solves that."

Step 7: RFP Flow + AI Normalization ⭐ TOP WOW

Vendors submit in ANY format (PDF, PPT, Word, Excel). AI reads all, normalizes pricing, side-by-side comparison

⭐ WOW: "10 vendors, 10 formats. AI normalizes all. No more manual spreadsheet hell."

Step 8: Evaluations

Assign evaluators to sections. Rate + shortlist. Delegation if unavailable. Download to Excel for offline.

Step 9: RFQ Creation

3 types: Standard, Grouped/Package (BOQ), Variant. Currency config: Any / Specific / Single + cross-currency.

Step 10: Sealed Bids

4 trigger types: Deadline, Foreclose, Post-technical-eval, Bid Opening Committee. Chinese Wall enforced.

⭐ WOW: "Physical sealed envelopes digitized with full audit trail"

Step 11: Vendor Alternatives

Vendor suggests better option with justification, pricing, docs. Buyer accepts or rejects in comparison view.

Step 12: AI Negotiation ⭐ TOP WOW

Target price + starting price per line item. AI drafts negotiation email with per-line-item reasoning. Multi-round back-and-forth.

⭐ WOW: "AI tells you what to aim for, what to start with, and WHY — based on YOUR spending history"

Step 13: Live Auction (Optional)

Real-time bidding. Multiple formats. Reserve prices, auto-extensions.

Step 14: Scenario Builder

Build multiple award scenarios (lowest cost, best quality, split award). Compare pros/cons. Select and auto-allocate.

Step 15: Award + Auto PO

Mix and match across vendors per line item. AI-generated justifications. On approval → AUTO-CREATES PO IN ERP.

Step 16: Audit Trail & Collaboration

Full logging. Internal + external chat. Thread-based conversations. Amendment versioning with change summaries.
